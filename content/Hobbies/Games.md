# Games

## Card Games

### Sleeping Queens

- Start of Game
	- 2 areas of 6 ( 3 x 2 ) - Green Ones
	- Deal a hand of 5 cards to each player - Red Ones
	- Rest of the cards in a pile + flip one over to start the game
	- Always draw a card to replace the one played. Ensure 5 cards remain in your hand
- During Play
	- If a potion is played, you need a magic wand to "Fix" it / Move on. Or a Queen may be stolen
	- If a jester is played, draw a number card, do eenie, meenie, minie, mo, until you've reached the number on the card. The player that it lands on gets to draw a queen
	- If playing a King, you can pick up a queen
	- If a Knight, you can steal a queen if they don't have a dragon
	- If a Rose Queen is drawn, draw a second Queen
- Winning the Game
	- Get 50 Queen points or 5 Queens

### Euchre

- [Euchre – Card Game Rules | Bicycle Playing Cards](https://bicyclecards.com/how-to-play/euchre/)
- 4 players
- Partners
- Play with 9 & above
- Each round can receive 1 or 2 points:
	- 1 point if you win 3 or more
	- 2 points if you win all 5
	- 2 points if the other team Euchres the team that called the Trump suite
- Deal hands of 5
- 4 left in the middle
- Flip the top card over
- Proceed around the table with each player "Calling Trump Suite" or "passing"
	- criteria to call Trump suite is whether you feel you can win 3 sets with the cards in your hand based on the suite of the top card
