## Glacier Express

- In Switzerland
- [PBS Train Documentary](https://www.pbs.org/video/glacier-express-dw28wi/)
- The Glacier Express is one of the most scenic train journeys in Switzerland.
- Known for its panoramic views of the Swiss Alps, connecting Zermatt and St. Moritz.
- Often referred to as "the slowest express train in the world," it offers breathtaking views over an eight-hour journey.
- The route includes numerous tunnels and bridges, showcasing engineering marvels amidst natural beauty.
- [Glacier Express - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glacier_Express)
