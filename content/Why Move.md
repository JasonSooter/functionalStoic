# Why Move

In 2018 when I started in tech, I immediately started a progression of an expansion of my worldview. This involved interacting more deeply with Europeans, people from France, Czech Republic, Germany and England. Some of them spoke more than one language and had been exposed to more places in the world through travel. This worldview expansion kicked off a series of events that won't allow me to unsee what I've seen and hear what I've heard and undo what I've done. 

I want my children to grow up with this expansive worldview.
