- Summary: The text discusses the journey to customer data maturity, which involves three levels: Foundational, Advancing, and Adaptive. Each level focuses on collecting, organizing, and utilizing customer data to improve engagement and drive business value. A Customer Data Platform (CDP) is essential for integrating data and creating unified customer profiles, enabling personalized marketing and efficient budget use.
