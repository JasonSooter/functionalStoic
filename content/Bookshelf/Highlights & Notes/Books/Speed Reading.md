### Step 1 — Preparation

1. What do I currently think I know?
	- Read w/ eyes & not mouth. Not much else
2. What do I want from this book?
	- Be able to read & comprehend at a significantly fast pace
	- Be able to sit and read a book in an afternoon / day / focused session
	- Read technical programming books at a faster pace
3. Why am I reading this book?
	- To sharpen a tool that I use very often. Arguably, my most valuable tool
4. What do I already know?
	- Not much!
5. What do I need to know?
	- How to read faster and learn to use a framework for processing books faster.

### Step 2 — Structure

1. What does it look like?
	- This book is divided into 7 sections. One for each day of the week.
2. Are there summaries or conclusions?
	- Yes.
3. Is the book all words, or are there any pictures?
	- There are some pictures. They seem to mainly be for decoration. No diagrams noticed
4. What size is the print?
	- Normal 12 pt or so
5. Is the text broken up in sections?
	- Yes.
		1. Read the front & back cover
		2. Determine Structure
		3. Eliminate parts you don't need
		4. Highlight areas that you do need
		5. Reaffirm decision to read the book!
