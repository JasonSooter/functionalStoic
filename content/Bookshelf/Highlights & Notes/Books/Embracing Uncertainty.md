## Summary

In [[Four Thousand Weeks]], #people/authors/oliver-burkeman mentions that we could approach life with the following attitude toward everything:

> [!quote] Not knowing what’s coming next—which is the situation you’re always in, with regard to the future—presents an ideal opportunity for choosing [[Curiosity]] (wondering what might happen next) over worry (hoping that a certain specific thing will happen next, and fearing it might not) whenever you can.
> — Oliver Burkeman (Location 2694)
