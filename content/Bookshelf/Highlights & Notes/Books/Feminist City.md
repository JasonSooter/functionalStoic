- Summary: "Feminist City" by Leslie Kern explores how urban spaces are designed to favor men, creating barriers for women. The book combines theory and personal experiences to highlight the challenges women face in cities, such as fear of violence and social exclusion. It calls for a reimagining of urban environments that better support women's needs and experiences.
