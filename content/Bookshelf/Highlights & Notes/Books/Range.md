## Summary

The book "Range" by David Epstein argues that generalists, who can apply knowledge creatively across different fields, often succeed better than specialists. It highlights that traditional education often emphasizes specialization, which can limit critical thinking and adaptability. The author encourages broad experiences and lateral thinking to foster creativity and problem-solving skills.
