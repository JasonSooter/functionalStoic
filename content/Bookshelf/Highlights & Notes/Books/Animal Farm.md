	 a: "Animal Farm" offers profound insights into the dynamics of power, corruption, and the complexities of societal ideals. - Summary: In "Animal Farm," the animals overthrow their human owner, Mr. Jones, hoping to create a better society. However, the pigs become corrupt leaders and start to resemble the humans they replaced. Eventually, the farm's slogan changes to reflect that some animals are more equal than others, highlighting the betrayal of their initial ideals.
