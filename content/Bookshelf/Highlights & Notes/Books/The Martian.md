## Summary

"The Martian" by Andy Weir follows astronaut Mark Watney as he struggles to survive on Mars after being stranded. He improvises solutions using limited resources while communicating with NASA for help. The story focuses on his determination and ingenuity as he works to stay alive until rescue.
