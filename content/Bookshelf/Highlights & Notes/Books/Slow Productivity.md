## Metadata

- Author: #people/authors/cal-newport
- Full Title: Slow Productivity Crash Course
- Category: #articles
- Summary: "Slow Productivity Crash Course" is a video introduction to the concept of slow productivity. The video explains that traditional definitions of productivity do not work well in the knowledge work sector, especially in the age of digital networks. The video introduces the idea of pseudo productivity, which is using visible activity as a proxy for useful labor. However, with the rise of technology, pseudo productivity became unsustainable and led to burnout and exhaustion. The video then introduces the concept of slow productivity, which is an alternative definition of productivity that focuses on doing fewer things, working at a natural pace, and obsessing over quality. The video provides practical advice for implementing these principles, such as using a public pull list to manage work.
