- **Retrospection** - I got a lot of value out of writing about what went well and what didn’t over the previous 6 months. The value mostly comes from considering what didn’t go well and thinking about how to do better the next time (this writing is a product of those reflections). ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jh9mqc5svdj16q6xvjq55knx))
- **Where do I want to be** - I break my life down into a few categories that are important to me (career, relationships, health, and hobbies). I review each category and think about how I’m doing in each. Then, I consider what it’d take to be completely satisfied in that area. This serves as the basis for my goal setting. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jh9mqf4ap8ptvena5th5dxvc))
