## Highlights

- If you haven't created your inner world, you rely on the outer world to fill the void.
	You need the trip, the attention, the nice apartment, the restaurants, the hobbies, the shows, the constant social outings.
	Without them, you feel empty. Maybe even depressed.
	But when your inner world is rich, the outer world becomes just background noise. You don't need it to feel full because you carry an inner sense of purpose.
	You have a mission, a goal, something that makes your inner world vibrant and alive. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq6p1b714nvv0sm9da4gv2h1))

> This might be one of the reasons I'm so Introverted. I have a very rich Inner World.

- Whatever it is, once you have something that makes you tick, the outer world loses its power over you. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq6p30jpk5tkx2aa61vr6gbe))
- That upcoming concert sounds cool, but a Friday night spent deep in your inner world? Even better. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq6p3j6dfpcg54bp42c42yzb))
