## Metadata

- Author: #people/authors/linnesby-maria
- Full Title: Indulgence Is Not the Same as Care
- Category: #articles
- Summary: The essay explores the difference between care and indulgence, emphasizing that care involves thoughtful decisions for overall well-being, while indulgence is simply doing what one wishes without harm. The author reflects on how people often confuse self-care with indulgence, failing to provide themselves the attentive care they readily give others. True care involves balancing indulgence with protection and requires recognizing one's own importance.
