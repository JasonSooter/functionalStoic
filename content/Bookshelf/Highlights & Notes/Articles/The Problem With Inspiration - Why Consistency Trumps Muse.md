## Highlights

- The Power of Consistency ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1a4hr3f1mmxtcjnmcdbzm))
- **Consistency is king.** By showing up regularly to your craft, you ensure a steady stream of work. Not everything you produce will be a masterpiece. But you're more likely to produce a masterpiece from producing a higher volume. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1ae95626wpe4j8ww63x4r))
- Most things in life are a numbers game, and art is no exception. Let's look at some of the greats: ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1aq1ehc9w8jyn7mj5emgh))
- **Pablo Picasso** created an estimated 147,800 pieces of art in his lifetime. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1avpm8p52pamjz5mwwdks))
- **Stephen King** has published 65 novels/novellas, including seven under the pen name Richard Bachman, and over 200 short stories ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1b0kv7g32hq1cf9g95v6g))
- **Paul McCartney** has written or co-written **over 30 songs** that reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100. He has also written hundreds more that never made it to the top. The Beatles themselves recorded **213 songs** during their main active years from 1962 to 1970. That's an impressive amount of creativity in just about 8 years. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1b7zt9jtt193fep4d98ft))
- **Thomas Edison** made 1,000 unsuccessful attempts at inventing the light bulb before he finally got it right. "I have not failed 1,000 times," he said. "I have successfully discovered 1,000 ways to NOT make a light bulb." ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1bt0wskm9naezt5nvszkn))
- These legends didn't wait for the muse to whisper something in their ear. They showed up, day after day, and did the work. Some of it sucked. Some of it was decent. And some of it changed the world. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1c1j6y3d56wab6mykkq4h))
- The Benefits of Regular Practice ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1cf0b3vw84jj12m3n9f6e))
	- [ ] Connect to Heroic Consistency. Regular practice is a loose synonym with a less hyperbolic name
- Regular practice leads to improvement. The more you engage with your craft, the better you become at it. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1cn5z1tf4ker4py7agf8b))
- The best artists aren't the ones with the most inspiring muses. They're the ones who show up day after day, doing the work, even when inspiration is playing hard to get. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j8b1d3tdba5e9djykd2j0xn3))
