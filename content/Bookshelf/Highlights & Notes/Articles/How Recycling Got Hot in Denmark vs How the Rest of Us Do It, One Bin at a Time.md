- Summary: In Denmark, recycling is ingrained in the national identity, with almost 90% of packaging being recycled. The Danish system includes separate bins for various materials and even innovative practices like waste-to-energy plants, but it also results in higher carbon emissions. Both Denmark and the UK aim to improve their recycling rates, with Denmark focusing on social responsibility and the UK working to simplify and enhance its system.
