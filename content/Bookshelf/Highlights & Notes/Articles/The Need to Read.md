- Summary: Reading is a crucial skill that cannot be easily replaced by more efficient ways of acquiring knowledge. While technology may offer alternatives, reading not only teaches us about a particular subject but also enhances our ability to write and think critically. Writing is not simply a means of conveying ideas, but also a way to develop and discover new insights. Without good reading and writing skills, individuals may struggle to solve complex, ill-defined problems. Therefore, it is essential to prioritize reading and cultivate strong reading and writing abilities to foster creativity and intellectual growth.
