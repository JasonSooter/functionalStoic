## Highlights
- Finishing a book a day is doable. An average adult can read roughly [250 words per minute](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0749596X19300786). That’s about one page every minute or so, depending on the book. At that rate, you can put away a 300-page book in five hours, give or take some trips to the coffeepot and bathroom. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k5rr9da7ety891gs8fyydpcc))
- Then let’s say you’re 40 right now and read two books a month, plus one extra, for a total of 25 books a year; you can expect to read a little more than eleven hundred additional books. If you’re 25, you’ve got fifteen hundred books to look forward to. The numbers go down as your age increases and up as your pace does. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k5rre1z4t10v5td13nnx8z4n))
- Whatever your particular age and pace, you can visualize the dwindling stacks you’ve got left with the adjoining graph. A middle aged power reader could reasonably hit another 4,000 titles on top of whatever they’ve already consumed. Of course, if you read while walking and wander into traffic, you’ll manage fewer. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k5rregxprrxm4ya51777r7f4))
- An eye toward the dwindling stacks might assist us in making better literary choices. “You should pick them wisely,” concluded economist Russ Roberts [after rehearsing similar numbers](http://www.econtalk.org/andy-matuschak-on-books-and-learning). And awareness of the ebbing piles might lend another advantage, as well, per Roberts: a reasonable justification for quitting bad books. As you know, [I fully endorse that move](https://www.millersbookreview.com/p/tbr-lol-dnf). Sticking with a snoozer is the literary equivalent of the sunk-cost fallacy. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k5rrfk9ma070v8tsgs143fwb))
- And then there are books we never quite finish reading but visit often, like our favorite coffeeshop or restaurant. I’ve never found the bottom of Montaigne’s *Essays*. Why would I want to? ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k5rrjbyfkntveazq9nmhwdfa))
