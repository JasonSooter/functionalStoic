## Highlights

- When a non-expert brings a strong point of view to a complex discussion, the words might not mean what they seem to mean.
	What might be being said is, “I’m worried. I’m afraid. I don’t understand. I am looking for solace.”
	Answering emotional word salad with logical insight doesn’t do anything at all for these feelings. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j6md1m1231n1evg8hf2fr15n))
- the argument you’re hearing isn’t based on what we know to be true, and doesn’t lead to a logical conclusion, it could be because it’s not actually seeking to be a productive discussion. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j6md2tstk3mrws0916gpt8d1))
- When in doubt, look for the emotions and desires behind the words. People often don’t want their arguments to be heard as much as they’re hoping their emotions will be. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j6md14y9nc2j5nhxna2fccgd))
