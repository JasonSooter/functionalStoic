## Metadata

- Author: #people/authors/david-brooks
- Full Title: How the Ivy League Broke America
- Category: #articles
- Summary: The Ivy League has created a system that favors wealthy families, making it hard for children from less affluent backgrounds to succeed. This focus on standardized testing and elite college admissions has stifled creativity and curiosity in education. To improve the meritocracy, we need to value traits like curiosity and social intelligence over traditional measures of intelligence.
