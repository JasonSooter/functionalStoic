- Summary: Helen Russell compares her children's experiences in the Danish and UK school systems, noting the differences in focus—Danish schools emphasize well-being and play, while UK schools stress academic rigor and early formal education. She highlights the benefits and drawbacks of both systems, expressing a desire for a hybrid model that balances academic challenge with happiness. Overall, she values the Danish approach for prioritizing children's happiness and social skills, despite recent declines in academic performance.
