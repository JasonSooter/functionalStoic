## Metadata

- Author: #people/authors/bram-adams
- Full Title: Issue 57: Can You Back Into a Second Brain With a Collection of Software Features?
- Category: #articles
- Summary: Bram Adams explores how external tools, or "second brains," can enhance our biological brains by addressing their shortcomings. His experiments with software, such as a Discord Bot and Chrome Extension, show mixed results due to differences in user habits and needs. He concludes that evolving technology and understanding user patterns are key to developing effective second brains.
