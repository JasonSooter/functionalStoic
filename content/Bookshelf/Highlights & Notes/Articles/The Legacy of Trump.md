## Highlights

- General Summary of his time in office
- Ended on a good note

> But we learned that we still want democracy

- A few statistics that were pointed out and were hard to refute
	- 2.3 million Americans lost health insurance … Before COVID
	- The US score on the Freedom House's (Human Rights org) annual index dropped from 90 (Obama era) → 86 (below Greece & Mauritius)
	- Refugees dropped per year from 85k → 12k
	- Unknown location of parents for 666 children seized at the border
	- National debt increased by $7 trillion (up 37%)
	- While in office, taxpayers & campaign donors handed over at least $8 million to the Trump Family
	- An astounding 25k lies were told by Trump while in office
