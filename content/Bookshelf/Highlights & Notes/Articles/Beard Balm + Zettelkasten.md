## Highlights

- your Zettelkasten isn’t about rigidly maintaining a library of thoughts. It’s about cultivating a dynamic network of connections.
	Rather than asking, “What category does this belong in?” ask, “Where do I want to bump into this note again?”
	This shifts your focus from classification to connection. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq5680z83raff4jwe9rc6rty))
- The magic happens when an idea collides and combines with seemingly unrelated ideas. This is the essence of creativity—pairing unlikely thoughts to spark new insights. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq568ezzj2z2fxrsdqnsnwjp))
- Write About the Connections ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq56905hk2wwzz5y4rn3sgy6))
- Connecting one note to another through links is a powerful first step, but it’s not the end.
	To truly work an idea into your Zettelkasten, you need to write about the connection. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq569600yzgervc00d7k2vs1))
- Writing bridges the connections and deepens your understanding. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq569a5gtjfbfwj8aahy4yvd))
