- Feedback analysis is a technique to improve judgment and discover your strengths. It could hardly be more simple:
	1. Write down what you think will happen whenever you make a key decision.
	2. In 9–12 months, compare what you predicted against what has happened.
	3. Repeat. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jj2nd7hfzv4mhddwdtad077j))

- He argues that we can only make our most significant contributions when we act from our strengths. Most people don't know their strengths, so the first step is to discover them. Feedback analysis helps you do this. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jj2ngkwwcw2p7x3be96stkba))
