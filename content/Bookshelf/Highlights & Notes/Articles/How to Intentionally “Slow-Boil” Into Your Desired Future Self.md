- **You’re not the same person you were 5 years ago:** It’s common and lazy to assume that people don’t change. We all change, slowly over time. Just think about yourself for a minute — are you the exact same person you were 5 years ago? Do you see the world the same way? Do you believe in exactly the same things? Do you have exactly the same habits? Do you want exactly the same things? Chances are, the person you were 5 years ago wanted things you no longer want. Also, you probably have different priorities and focus than you had 5 years ago. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01hnvfc61kq5wea7dzrsrm9dn2))
