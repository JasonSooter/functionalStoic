## Highlights

- Everything can be taken from a man but one thing: the last of the human freedoms—to choose one’s attitude in any given set of circumstances, to choose one’s own way.
	— Viktor E. Frankl, [*Man’s Search for Meaning*](http://amzn.to/2rLQQwt) ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6d4shqgwd19gznb2e71x0))
- That means ***we don’t have to limit ourselves to the paths offered by others.*** ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6eb8fzb3190h994766a7e))
- There is no rulebook to life.
	[You’re free to live how you want](https://www.highexistence.com/hunter-s-thompsons-monumental-letter-on-how-to-find-meaning-and-purpose-in-life/). We don’t have to fulfill any cosmic demands from above, and there is no high score for which to strive. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6gwqj9cvhqc6qe6vs9j72))
- We’re just here for a time, we do a bunch of stuff, and then we’re gone. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6hc1tsnwre9c2snxjym22))
- It’s understandable if you feel uncomfortable about that; I know I do from time to time. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6hmyr3b8nw340f1nsjxwb))
- Often we are driven by a certain purpose. We get out of bed in the morning to get something done. The idea that there’s no grand purpose for life, no ultimate thing to “get done,” can take away the foundations on which we base our lives. It forces us to ask the question: “Well, then, *what’s the point of it all?”* ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6k0fke5pp1x4gnw6c9f94))
- “The point” is whatever you make it to be, and the fact that we have the freedom to choose in that regard *astounds me*. It is a beautiful truth that I absolutely revere. **I get to decide my life’s purpose, and you get to decide yours**. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6khd47k3g961jpa57mjp2))
- Life is [marvelously meaningless](https://www.highexistence.com/stanley-kubricks-epic-quote-on-the-meaninglessness-of-life/). Meaninglessness leaves an empty space in life. We are blessed with the fortunate position of filling that space with *whatever the fuck we want*. We work with the medium of free will upon the canvas of life’s empty space to create and express ourselves. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6mg0a71cekrk3nf1mcdds))
- So stop listening to all the commercials. Cast away the definitions of success that have been conditioned into you. Don’t worry about what your friends will think, worry about your own moral conscience. What kind of life will *you* feel at peace with? Ask *your own* heart and mind what’s right for *you*. And then, most importantly, **go live your answer!** ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j0k6mz90jv70h4wgtbsmh5dz))
