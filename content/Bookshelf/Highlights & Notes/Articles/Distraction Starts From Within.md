## Highlights

- A few years later, after seeing the consequences of his work, Nir turned his focus to fighting distraction. He wanted to help people fight the addictive products he literally helped create. His second book, *Indistractable*, is one of my all-time favorites. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd2rchmwntwsvpj15w9fpy))
	- Tags: #add-book-to-read
- Nir shares a useful protocol for identifying your internal triggers and working through them. It’s part journaling, part meditation: ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd4xnnqrkg7bg6sv6ps2xv))
- First keep a notebook or digital note handy while working. You’ll use this to track your internal triggers. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd53nzqspgchjgjtmb3x1t))
- As you work, pay attention to when you seek out distraction. **At that very moment, write down the thoughts and feelings you’re having. This is your internal trigger.** Write down the feelings in a very objective way, as if you’re a scientist observing a bug. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd580nrqa293bj2qr1vd5y))
- After writing down the internal trigger, sit and breath intentionally for 10 minutes (I.e., meditate) ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd5f4rcp8rnzqqdsw4h4j3))
- After the 10 minutes is up, you now have permission to partake in the distraction *if you still want to.* Giving yourself this option is important. 99% of the time, the urge to distract yourself will have passed. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd5nwqpg1w5egr6jv8gz7s))
- I find when doing this protocol that I can take 10 deep breaths instead of 10 minutes and achieve the same effect with less time. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd6a6y0xdwnr6n1na3a3fy))
- The important part is stopping to observe your internal triggers. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd79ap35ddaec4x6s6xhft))
- Slow down, observe your thoughts, take a few deep breaths, and let the wave pass.
	Then get on with your work. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jmtd7zcj08a2n8e4smg48jqw))
