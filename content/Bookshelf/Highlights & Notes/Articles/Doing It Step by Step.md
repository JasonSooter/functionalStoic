## Highlights

- I was surprised to discover that for many AI questions, if you add, “please figure this out step by step,” the AI will [provide](https://every.to/also-true-for-humans/7-22) a dramatically more accurate and useful answer. This works on simple questions like, “how many times does the letter ‘r’ appear in the word ‘strawberry'” to much more complex issues.
	It probably won’t surprise you to consider that this works even better for people. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j6e3ghdrm6yq8ab498svw0bs))
