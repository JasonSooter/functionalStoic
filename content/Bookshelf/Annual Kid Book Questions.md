# Annual Kid Book Questions

1. Favorite Character
	- Who stood out the most? Why?
2. Most Memorable Moment
	- What part of the story do you remember most vividly?
3. Biggest Surprise
	- Was there a twist or something unexpected?
4. Key Lesson or Message
	- What did the book teach you or make you think about?
5. Favorite Quote or Line
	- Share one line or dialogue you really liked.
6. Connection to Real Life
	- Did something in the book remind you of your life or the world around you?
7. Best Illustration or Description
	- If the book had pictures, which one was your favorite? If not, what scene was described best?
8. Something You’d Change
	- If you could change one part of the story, what would it be?
9. Funniest or Saddest Part
	- Which part made you laugh the most or feel emotional?
10. Favorite Author’s Writing Style
	- Did you enjoy how the author told the story? What made their style unique?
11. Character You’d Be Friends With
	- Who would you pick as your friend and why?
12. Favorite Setting
	- Which place or time period in the book did you like most?
13. Something New You Learned
	- Did the book teach you anything interesting or surprising?
14. Most Relatable Character
	- Which character could you relate to the most and why?
15. Comparison to Other Books
	- How does this book compare to others you’ve read?
16. Alternate Ending Idea
	- If you could rewrite the ending, how would you do it?
17. Best Supporting Character
	- Who helped the story or main character in the best way?
18. Title Rating
	- Was the title a good fit for the book? Would you rename it?
19. Would You Recommend It?
	- Would you tell someone else to read it? Why or why not?
20. A Question for the Author
	- If you could ask the author one thing about the book, what would it be?
