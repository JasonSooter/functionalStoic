## How Have You Changed in the Last 5 Years?

#people/authors/ben-hardy mentioned this in [[Ben Hardy-How to Intentionally “Slow-Boil” Into Your Desired Future Self-Highlights|How to Intentionally "Slow-Boil" Into Your Desired Future Self]]

You’re not the same person you were 5 years ago. It’s common and lazy to assume that people don’t change. We all change, slowly, over time.

Just think about yourself for a minute — are you the same person you were 5 years ago?
- Do you see the world in exactly the same way?
- Do you believe in exactly the same things?
- Do you have exactly the same habits?
- Do you want exactly the same things?
- Chances are, the person you were 5 years ago wanted things you no longer want. Also, you probably have different priorities and focus than you had 5 years ago.
