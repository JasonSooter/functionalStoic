## Ellie's Awesome Email While in the Bahamas

4/17/36
Dear Father,

Since you have been gone sooooo many years. I thought it would be nice to fill you in on what is happening. First of all I have graduated  from MIT with a degree in aeronautical engineering and have taken a job at NASA which now has a branch in northern Colorado.  Surprisingly I now love coffee:). [[Mac]] is now a successful Pro Basketball point guard in [[Denmark]] and is living with his girlfriend Astrid. [[Poppy|Poppy]] is competing in the 2036 Indian Olympics. And she is living with her boyfriend Ryan .Mom has become a cat lady. She now has 5 cats and they sleep in your part of your bed. She also continues to volunteer for the send town races and they have started a cat section just for her cats. Kevin is now married to a women named Olivia They now have 3 year old quintuplets: Graham, Griffin, Gemma, Gabriela, and Georgia. Mom loves to read to them.  In your memory, we have read the Harry Potter books one year at a time and we have just recently finished the Fantastic Beast series. We also finished the Star Wars saga, but that was 7 years ago. And also in your memory [[Poppy|Poppy]] has slept with Gnarly every night since you left for the Bahamas.

I love and miss you,

Ellie Cherie Sooter
