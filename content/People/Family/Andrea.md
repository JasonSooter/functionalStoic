## ME (Written About herself)

- #🗓️/2006/08/20
- Auditing
- Professor Elliot

My name is Andrea Cherie Lyons, on October 14th of this year I will marry the man I love, Jason Sooter.

Jason & I currently own two businesses, so I will be gaining not only a husband, but a business partner as well. It’s not always easy being business partners, but we wouldn’t have it any other way! His primary business is remodeling, he loves to create fancy kitchens, bathrooms, and tile floors. Sometimes I even help him do tile -floors and backsplashes. I run our other company which is a small vending operation. We have 60 small candy machines and 1 pop machine (we have 7 more that I am trying to find places for right now). Our goal is to have 200 small candy machines, those 200 machines should more than cover our living expenses and I'll not have to work outside of Sooter Companies, Inc.

I have always dreamed of owning my own company, so he caught my attention when we first me when he told me, "I own my own company, I don't ever want to work for someone else." Many times I have said the same thing! We are a perfect match for each other, and I am very thankful for him in my life. He is very much the visionary and I am the one who puts it all on paper and helps to keep. the money straight.

I enjoy photography, especially children! In the last year, I've had two of my photo's published, one in Tulsa People Magazine and the other in an international ministry's calendar. Both of those publications were very exciting to receive! My parents first put a camera into my hands when I was 6 years old, and I haven't put it down since! I currently have a 7.2 megapixel Sony Cybershot, and it is by far my favorite gadget!

I also love to cook, which is a blessing for Jason. He is 6’5” and has a huge appetite! I’ve only been cooking for about a year and a half, but I’ve really come to like it. I don’t know how to cook very many items ,but Jason sings my praises anyways! I always make a big mess when I cook and then Jason cleans up the dishes afterward, it’s a nice system.

Several goals we have for our life together are to have 3 kids, to build our dream home on several acres, and to continue owning & starting businesses. We desire the freedom to [[travel]], specifically to the beach at least once a year, as well as plenty of [[camping]] and canoeing.
