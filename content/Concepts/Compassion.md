# Compassion

Quote from [[The Ezra Klein Show|Ezra Klein]] #people/authors/ezra-klein

> I often think in politics you face this implicit sense that compassion or concern is zero sum, that to care about one group or one issue is to care less about another. I just don’t believe that. I actually think there’s evidence is not true. And maybe I can talk about that in an Ask Me Anything episode sometime.
> But compassion, it’s not measured out in teaspoons from a cup. It’s quite the opposite. I think it’s much more of a habit, something we get better at, something we have more capacity for the more we practice it"
> [source](https://www.nytimes.com/2023/03/10/opinion/ezra-klein-podcast-richard-reeves.html#:~:text=I%20often%20think,we%20practice%20it)
