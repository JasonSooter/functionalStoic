# Integrity

## Summary

Integrity refers to the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles. It involves consistently adhering to ethical standards and being truthful in one's actions and words. People with integrity are reliable, trustworthy, and take responsibility for their actions, even when it is difficult. This quality is essential in both personal and professional settings, as it builds trust and respect from others. Integrity also involves aligning one’s behavior with their values, standing up for what is right, and maintaining consistency in actions over time. Overall, integrity is a cornerstone of character that guides individuals in making morally sound decisions.
