## Content

[[Curiosity]] fuels customer discovery. T-shaped people ensure its effectiveness. Teams that create customer experiences, such as onboarding or lead capture, typically consist of generalists and specialists. When these teams are curious and eager to learn from customers, it can lead to insightful and innovative results. However, when a T-shaped person leads the customer discovery process, I've noticed a substantial increase in the program's effectiveness.

T-shaped people have:
- Deep knowledge in one area
- A Wide range of skills
- Creative and new ideas
- Are good at working with others
- Understand different parts of the business
- Helps others learn and grow

Generalists will help unlock the work of experts by seeing across the business. T-Shape people drive everyone forward by connecting both vertical and horizontal dots.
