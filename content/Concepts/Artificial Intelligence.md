## Norway's Sovereign Wealth Fund Experiment

In [Norway's $2 trillion wealth fund ran a 12-month AI experiment](https://x.com/karlmehta/status/1947284189461782807/), a few key points were made

> [!quote] AI became their analytical partner, not their replacement. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k26h7k8tveehrrqxbmjp8hyz))

Summary: Norway's $2 trillion wealth fund used AI to save 213,000 hours and boost productivity by 20%. The AI helped spot human biases that hurt investment decisions and made its use mandatory for all employees. This led to $100 million in savings and made the fund a leader in AI-driven asset management.

Tangen's framework for other executives:

> [!quote] Don't make AI voluntary. Measure everything. Start with specific use cases.
> ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k26h8f5ar8vndz7pe4swg479))

The strategy is clear: Build capabilities systematically. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k26h93hypvc9s538m95wtdz0))
