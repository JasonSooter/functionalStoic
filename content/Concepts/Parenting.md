## On Balancing the Parental Workload

In [Workaday or Novelty Parent](https://mshelenrussell.substack.com/p/workaday-vs-novelty-parenting-the), Helen Russel, #people/authors/helen-russell, conveys the result of splitting the domestic load
- More balanced relationships
- Fewer feelings of resentment
- Better understanding of what it takes to keep a family running smoothly.

She uses data from an OECD study that found countries with stronger support systems for working parents, like [[Denmark]], tend to have higher levels of well-being. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jq2gex1w2mjkr2jwyv9zr69p))
