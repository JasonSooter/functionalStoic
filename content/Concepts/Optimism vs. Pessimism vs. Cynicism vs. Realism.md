# Optimism Vs. Pessimism Vs. Cynicism Vs. Realism

## Optimism

> [!quote] Optimism is a performance-enhancing drug that's both legal and free.
> — #people/authors/fs-farnam-street ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jpy0rrznb9antmbcd90bwed2))

### Optimism Should Be An Active Role

Optimism is dangerous; many people confuse it for hope or toxic positivity. But Thompson’s definition of optimism is more of a call to action than a prayer:

> [!quote] Optimism is not… believing arc of history always bends towards justice. Optimism is recognizing the future is soft clay, and you have two hands.

We need to be optimistic, which means we need to take an active role in molding the future we want. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jrjq5wbym9h40fs3p8n9s0m2))

### Indefinite Vs. Definite Optimism

Author: #people/authors/daniel-mccormick
Source: [Weekly Dose of Optimism 81](https://www.notboring.co/p/weekly-dose-of-optimism-81)
Date: #🗓️/2024/02/17

- **Definition:** A positive outlook on life, characterized by a belief that good things will happen.
- **Examples:**
	- "I'm confident I'll succeed in this job interview."
	- "I believe tomorrow will be a great day."
- **Comparative Differences:**
	- More positive than pessimism, cynicism, and realism.
	- Less grounded in reality than realism.
	- May lead to unrealistic expectations.
- ***Indefinite Optimism***
	- is a mindset where individuals believe the future will be better but lack a clear vision or concrete plan for making it so. Indefinite Optimists do things like “listen to customers”, “build things people want,” and “run lean startups” — not necessarily bad at face value *(or for creating shareholder value)* but lead only to incremental outcomes. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01hpx9mf1xemjc0ctew02fwkj3))
- Definite Optimism
	- Definite Optimists, on the other hand, not only envision a better future but actively work towards it with specific goals and strategies. These are folks working on Hard Startups that are creating new markets vs. slicing off value from existing ones. These folks are building a new future, not just an incrementally better one. In Asparouhova’s view, after a couple of decades, Silicon Valley is just now returning to definite optimism, and we tend to agree. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01hpx9n84b6aszjpngtpgwmdcf))

## Pessimism

- **Definition:** A negative outlook on life, characterized by a belief that bad things will happen.
- **Examples:**
	- "I'm sure I'll fail that test."
	- "The world is a terrible place."
- **Comparative Differences:**
	- More negative than optimism, cynicism, and realism.
	- May lead to low self-esteem and depression.
	- Can be less motivating than other perspectives.

## Cynicism

- **Definition:** A skeptical and distrustful attitude, characterized by a belief that people are selfish and motivated by greed.
- **Examples:**
	- "Politicians are all corrupt."
	- "Love is just a facade."
- **Comparative Differences:**
	- More negative than optimism and realism.
	- Less negative than pessimism.
	- May lead to social isolation and [[Resentment|bitterness]].

## Realism

- **Definition:** A balanced and accurate view of life, characterized by an understanding of both the good and bad aspects.
- **Examples:**
	- "I'm aware of the challenges, but I believe I can overcome them."
	- "Life isn't always easy, but there are also many joys."
- **Comparative Differences:**
	- More balanced than optimism, pessimism, and cynicism.
	- May be seen as less exciting or engaging than other perspectives.
	- Can provide a foundation for resilience and adaptability.
