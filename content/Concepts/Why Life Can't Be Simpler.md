# Why Life Can’t Be Simpler

---
_source:_ <https://fs.blog/2020/10/why-life-cant-be-simpler/>
_local link:_ [[]]
_reference:_ [[]]
_previous note:_

---
_content_
- This article put "Complexity" into a very concrete form. It framed complexity in the same way that energy is explained in the sense that complexity cannot be removed, just shifted from one place to another. The con·tin·u·um being:
	- `User of Complex tool, feature < --- > Creator of Complex tool, feature`
- The article outlines how there is only shifting of complexity from one entity to the other. i.e
	- User has complex interface to tool and creator has minimal complexity to maintain
	- User has elegantly simple interface, but creator has spent significant effort and taken [[Independence|responsibility]] of the complexity involved

---

_questions & thoughts_
- How can I tie this to Flex?
- Proxy is a tool that removed complexity for customers (Masked Phone Numbers), but customers were able to build the feature previously with existing Twilio tools, but took on the [[Independence|responsibility]] of managing their own complexity
- I very much like the study of complexity. Understanding of large systems and the processes required to make them work
- This article gave some concrete perspective to many examples of the easy (and simple-minded) view by many that building simple solutions should be:
	- easy
	- involves removing infrastructure required to enable simple solutions
