# Be Quiet Rich

What's The Quiet Rich?

“Rich” doesn’t mean just having money. The Quiet Rich also want to be:

- rich in time
- rich in [[health]]
- rich in self-awareness
- rich in relationships

“Quiet” doesn’t mean living in monk mode. The Quiet Rich have:

- quiet minds (free of self-criticism or regret).
- quiet confidence (they’re self-assured, not arrogant).
- quiet goals (they move in silence, and don't desire mainstream fame).
