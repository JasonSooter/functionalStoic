In [How to Have Better Habits in 2024](https://ryanholiday.net/how-to-have-better-habits-in-2024/), #people/authors/ryan-holiday mentions a conversation with [Dr. Edith Eger](https://dailystoic.com/drs-edith-eger-and-marianne-engle-on-forgiving-over-and-over-again/) where he mentions feeling guilty about how having lost touch with someone and only recently reconnected with them. She cut him off and told him she could give him a gift that would solve that guilt right now.

> [!quote] I give you a sentence, one sentence—*if I knew then what I know now, I would have done things differently*. That’s the end of that “Guilt is in the past, and the one thing you cannot change is the past."
> —Dr. Edit Eger ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j4s4mby8e7r0p90f2bk5afx5))
