In [How to Navigate the Tariff Circus](https://www.mrmoneymustache.com/2025/06/13/tariffs-and-the-economy/), #people/authors/mr-money-mustache, makes some points that connect well with the Project Ostrich POV

There are two solutions to this:

1. Either ignore the media completely and focus on your own life
2. Become a subject matter expert on things you really care about, and then read the original sources whenever you are keen to learn about something.

I mostly practice option #1, but as a science and technology nerd, I get into #2 in just the areas I find most interesting.

And it’s remarkable how the more deeply you understand a subject, the more you see just how *wrong* most media stories are about your area of expertise. Which means they’re probably pretty wrong about almost everything. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jya98x337yescyw8pde3z5p4))

So as always, with this lesson learned, it’s time to shut down that phone and laptop, exhale all our worries, and get back outside with your real-life family and friends. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jya99bpgt1p5k2rb9neafe4g))
