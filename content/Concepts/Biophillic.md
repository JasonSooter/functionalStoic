# Biophillic

> The Need For Nature

Biophilic means relating to or characterized by biophilia, which is the human tendency to interact with or be associated with nature. The biophilia hypothesis suggests that humans have an innate tendency to seek connections with nature and other forms of life. 

Biophilic design is a methodology for designing buildings and landscapes that nurtures the relationship between people and nature. Biophilic design emphasizes the connection between humans and nature and is used to create environments that promote health, productivity, and creativity. 
Edward O. Wilson introduced and popularized the biophilia hypothesis in his book, Biophilia
