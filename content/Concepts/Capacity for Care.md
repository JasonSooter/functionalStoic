## Content

In [I Don't Care](https://seths.blog/2025/03/i-dont-care/), Seth Godin, #people/authors/seth-godin points out that aspects of care that fit nicely with our capacity to care

- Care requires time and effort, and we can’t care about everything, all the way, all the time. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jpg07n2fte1afspewfgwtq98))
- If you’re prepared to care about every element of your work, then you also have to decide to *not* care about something else. Because caring equally about everything means that someone else cares more than you do about something. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jpg080h6wc8zzqa1g02nprbm))
- Deciding to care also requires you do the hard work of not caring. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jpg07bgeg8dhj9v96wxcbvf6))
