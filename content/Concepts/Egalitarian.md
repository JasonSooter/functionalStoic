# Egalitarian

## Summary

Egalitarianism is a philosophical and social concept centered around the idea of equality, particularly prioritizing equal rights and opportunities for all individuals. It opposes hierarchical and unequal structures in society, advocating for the fair distribution of resources and opportunities to ensure that everyone is treated with equal respect and fairness. Egalitarian principles can be applied across various dimensions, such as political, economic, social, and legal contexts, aiming to remove barriers that may lead to discrimination or privilege based on race, gender, economic status, or other characteristics. In essence, egalitarianism seeks to create a society in which all individuals have an equal chance to succeed and participate fully in civic life.
