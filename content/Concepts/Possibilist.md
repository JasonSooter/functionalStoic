# Possibilist

## Summary

[[Hans Rosling]] described himself as a Possibilist in his book [[Hans Rosling|How I Learned to Understand the World]]. He described it as someone who combines a realistic understanding of the world with an optimistic outlook on future possibilities. Possibilists rely on data to maintain a balanced perspective, focusing on actionable solutions and opportunities for improvement.
