# David Rumsey Map Collection

## Summary

The David Rumsey Map Collection is a digital collection of historical maps, globes, and atlases from around the world. The collection includes over 150,000 maps and related images, dating from the 16th to the 21st century. Users can explore the collection online through the website, which offers high-resolution images and detailed metadata for each item.

The collection is curated by David Rumsey, a map collector and philanthropist who has been collecting maps for over 30 years. Rumsey's goal is to make these historical maps accessible to the public and to promote the study and appreciation of cartography.

The collection is organized by geographic region, time period, and subject matter, making it easy for users to browse and search for specific maps. In addition to maps, the collection also includes atlases, globes, and other cartographic materials.

The David Rumsey Map Collection is a valuable resource for historians, researchers, educators, and anyone with an interest in maps and [[geography]]. The website also offers educational resources, such as lesson plans and interactive tools, to help users learn more about cartography and the history of maps.

Overall, the David Rumsey Map Collection is a rich and diverse resource that provides a unique glimpse into the past through the lens of cartography. Whether you're a map enthusiast or a history buff, this collection is sure to offer something of interest.
