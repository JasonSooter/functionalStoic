This concept was kicked off because I came across two sources of inspiration in the same day:

1. Video by Shu Omi - [Knowing vs. Understanding](https://youtu.be/GL6Z1DTJ-LQ)
	- He also mentioned the #people/authors/richard-feynman ***"Technique for Writing"*** which is likely worth looking into further
2. The book How to Read a Book dug deep into the distinction between:
	- Reading for Information
	- Reading for Understanding

I think I've felt & thought this way for many years, so I want to dig deeper into the idea and use it for how I think in the future.
