## From a LinkedIn Post

No one is going to watch the 30-60 minute call recording you send post-meeting.

Instead, record a 3-5 minute custom video, covering:
- Use cases discussed
- Value props + differentiation
- Product demo
- Next steps

That’s a video that’ll get watched. That’s a video that’ll get shared internally. That’s how you build an async groundswell.

Yes, it takes time. Yes, it’s not easy. Yes, it’s worth it.
