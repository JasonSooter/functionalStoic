# Personal Mission Statement

## Changelog

- #🗓️/2008/02/02 As of February, 2nd 2008
	- Silence
		- Speak only when spoken to. Don’t talk about yourself unless asked specific questions. Don’t divulge personal details unless there will be gain for the listener.
	- Listener
		- Be a wonderful listener. Do not finish other people’s sentences. Do not jump to conclusions. Listen to what people have to say and the tone they tell it in. Much can be learned if you listen to what is said from body language
	- Other People
		- Remember Names, Dates, Appointments, and all details that people tell you. Write them down so you can recall them if you need to.
	- Moderation
		- Do nothing in extreme quantities.
	- Cleanliness
		- Tolerate no uncleanliness in body, mind, clothes, or habitation
	- Let the First Act of every morning involve me reciting the below followed by mission statement:
		- “I shall not fear anyone on earth” —Ghandi
		- “I shall only fear God”, Ghandi
		- Great Things are ahead if I will be determine, patient and steadfast.
	- Then do a Today’s Winning Key and Read the Bible
	- Run a Successful business that is profitable, beneficial to people with hardships, and will provide a good life for my family.
	- Work with people that have been dealt hardships and help them to get off on the right foot
- #🗓️/2007/03/31 As of March 31, 2007
	- I discipline myself effectively.
	- I intensely pursue goals grounded on principles and integrity
	- I organize my daily schedule around balanced, principled priorities
	- I listen twice as much as I speak
	- I am a self-manager, effectively managing the affairs of my life.
	- I have an abundance of life, but only a limited amount of time, so i stay focused on living a fascinating life.
	- I focus on relationships that accomplish my goals and that are in line with my principles.
	- I succeed at home first and foremost because my wife is very precious
	- I am a good steward of all that is entrusted to me.
	- I maintain a healthy body through healthy diet, exercise and effort
	- I maintain a healthy mind by carefully guarding what is seen by my eyes
	- I hold honesty as the highest of principles and do not compromise on any.
	- I always remember that people have lives, problems, and worries that affect their actions and responses.
	- I obtain counsel from as many as possible, but always remember to 1st qualify the counselors and to weigh their advice against my principles, priorities, and own judgement.
	- I am sincere yet decisive, valuing others advice and help, but being quick to maintain staying on track.
	- I am a visionary. I must maintain that vision with excellent counsel.
	- I realize that success in my life will be through great vision and day in, day out resilient diligence.
	- I plan tomorrow’s work today.
	- I stay on schedule and on budget.
	- I am productive when I must wait because it is valuable time wasted otherwise
	- I always have a sense of humor. I laugh at my mistakes and am ok if others do as well.
	- I am not afraid of mistakes! However, I am afraid of not proactively correcting the cause of my mistakes in creative and constructive ways.
	- I am careful not to give much consideration to criticism given with no solution.
	- I am an influencer so my success is a reflection of the success of all the people I influence.
	- I am orderly, organized and clean.
	- I wake up by 6 am everyday because I have goals to accomplish and a lot of life to enjoy.
	- I concentrate all my ability on the task at hand.
