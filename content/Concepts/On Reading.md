- 2. Choose Books You Can Use Instantly ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j5p1dkp3bd4d0tckf3p18m1k))
- One way to improve reading comprehension is to choose books you can immediately apply. Putting the ideas you read into action is one of the best ways to secure them in your mind. ## The Practice of Re-Reading Books

In [How to Have Better Habits in 2024](https://ryanholiday.net/how-to-have-better-habits-in-2024/), #people/authors/ryan-holiday promoted the concept of not just reading books, but also re-reading books. He says, I've always loved [[Stoicism#Seneca]]'s line

> [!quote] You must linger among a limited number of master-thinkers, and digest their works, if you would derive ideas which shall win firm hold in your mind
> —[[Stoicism#Seneca|Seneca]] ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j4s481semem8sfq895tg1cwn))

When I re-read, I learn things I wasn’t ready to learn before, and I gain things that I missed the first time. We never step in the same river twice, [[Stoicism#Marcus Aurelius|Marcus Aurelius]] said. The books don’t change, but you and your circumstances do. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01j4s4b6hn0swby5av532jp1r7))
