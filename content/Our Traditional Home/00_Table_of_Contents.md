## About This Blog

This blog chronicles the life of Andrea and Jason Sooter, documenting their journey through marriage, career changes, starting a business, pregnancy loss, and the everyday joys and challenges of traditional home life. The posts span from 2010 to 2011 and capture their authentic experiences with faith, family, friends, and creating a meaningful life together.
