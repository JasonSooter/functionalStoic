## Legend (decoded fast)

- **Solid red:** off-street **paved** multi-use path (two-way, family-friendly)
- **Magenta dashed (along a road):** on-street **bike lane/track**
- **Red dotted:** **quiet 30 km/h** neighborhood route (residential grid)
- **Brown dashed:** **unpaved** forest/field path (gravel/roots; great with 35–45 mm tires)
- **Orange lines:** major/arterial roads (higher stress)
- **Green symbols:** **signalized crossings/junctions** (use these to cross big roads)
- **Small red arrows:** **one-way** streets
- **“No bike” symbol:** cycling **prohibited**
- **Chevron “teeth” marks:** **steep** segment (climbs into Schlossberg/Roßkopf/Günterstal)

---

## How to Read it

- **Purpose & coverage:** East-side Freiburg (Altstadt → Herdern/Zähringen edge; Waldsee/Littenweiler; Günterstal). Schools labeled; routes connect residential grids to schools.
- **Choose by stress level:** Solid red (lowest), magenta dashed (moderate, next to traffic), red dotted (calm back-streets but more stops), brown dashed (surface risk).
- **Crossing strategy:** String routes via **green** crossings to hop big barriers (B31/Schwarzwaldstraße, Habsburgerstraße, Basler Straße).
- **Grade awareness:** Stay on the **Dreisam** river corridor for flat E↔W. Expect bitey gradients into the hills.

---

## Practical Takeaways

- **Flattest E↔W trunk:** **Dreisamradweg** (long solid-red ribbon along the river); minimal crossings; kid default.
- **N↔S kid-grid:** Use **red-dotted** neighborhood links through Herdern/Neuburg/Wiehre; drop to the river path for speed/safety.
- **Hilly fun:** **Brown-dashed** webs above Herdern and Günterstal for XC/MTB; save for bigger tires and dry days.
- **Risk pockets to respect:**
	- **B31/Schwarzwaldstraße** crossings (fast traffic)
	- **Habsburgerstraße** turning traffic at nodes
	- **Altstadt** “no-bike” pockets + one-ways on dotted routes

---

## Neighborhood Cheat Sheet

- **Wiehre ↔ Altstadt:** Back-street dotted grid → green crossing → short magenta link → Altstadt edge.
- **Waldsee/Littenweiler ↔ City:** Ride **Dreisamradweg**; exit via green nodes.
- **Günterstal ↔ City:** Forest edges + selected magenta links; avoid steep chevrons unless you want a workout.

## Gear & Setup (lightweight)

- Lights front/rear; reflective bands for dawn/dusk.
- Bell mandatory; gloves for winter mornings.
- Tire choice: 32–35 mm slick (paved-only) vs 38–45 mm semi-slick (mixed).
