### Danish Sleeping Shelter

- #people/authors/helen-russell mentioned nailing the final plank onto our very own Danish-style sleeping shelter—a love letter in wood to the life we’ve left and the values we’re trying to keep hold of.
	- For the uninitiated, these shelters dot the rural landscape in Denmark, nestled into forests and alongside fjords. They’re part wild cabin, part architectural installation, with angled lines, the universal raised platform and a deep respect for nature knotted through every plank. Designed to maximise *friluftsliv*, it’s rough and ready and occasionally uncomfortable, but in a way that makes you remember you’re alive. ([View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01jtc21dcees7vccwwn68atv17))
