## Changelog

## Resources

## Download a Whole Playlist to Mp3

```bash
yt-dlp -ciw --extract-audio --audio-format mp3 'https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q6oeeUdDYVM&list=PLScGxr2tqwpYsZiPn0n4YM34I3vwSrUbQ'
```
