## Arc Command Pallet Options

- New Window opens up a second Arc window, with your Spaces and tabs.
- Blank Window opens up a blank Arc window.
- New Incognito Window opens up a blank Arc window in Incognito Mode.
- Little Arc opens a Little Arc window.
- Toggle Sidebar opens or closes your sidebar.
- Reopen Last Closed Tab reopens the last tab you closed.
- Copy Current URL copies the web address of your current tab.
- Clear Today clears all tabs in the Today section.
- Reveal Tab in Sidebar reveals the location of your active tab in the sidebar.
- Reset Space resets all Pinned Tabs in your Space back to their original URL.
- Go Back in History navigates "back" on the current webpage.
- Go Forward in History navigates "forward" on the current webpage.
- Downloads opens the Downloads panel of the Arc Library.
- Quit Arc quits the browser (say it ain't so!).
- Find… allows you to search page for keywords
- Close Tab closes current Pinned tab.
- Archive Tab archives current tab.
- Minimize Window minimizes current window.
- Close Window closes current window.
- New Space creates a new Space in Arc.
- Select Next Space moves your sidebar to your next Space (one to the right).
- Select Previous Space moves your sidebar to your previous Space (one to the left).
- Focus On [Space Namel switches sidebar focus to the Space of your choice.
- Pin to [Space Namel pins your current tab.
- Pin to Folder Namel pins a tab to a specific Folder.
- Pin to [New Folder] pins a tab to a new Folder.
- Unpin Tab unpins current tab.
- Favorite Tab adds a tab to Favorites.
- New Folder creates a new Folder.
- Rename Current Tab lets you rename your tab.
- Duplicate Current Tab duplicates your tab.
- Reset Tab resets to your original Pinned tab.
- Replace Pin with Current Page replaces your Pinned tab with the page you're currently on.
