# 2017 Journal Entries

## #🗓️/2017/01/26

I got back last night from spending a week in Pacific Grove, CA (Monterey, CA area). [[Kolt Kane|Kolt]] had invited Kelly and I out to work on tiling 2 bathrooms on his project. It was a air-fare paid trip to CA with $1000 bucks as well. It would have been silly to turn it down so with almost no notice, I hopped on a plane the next day.

It was a great deal of work but also a great deal of fun. I remembered why I jumped on making picture frames. Being on my knees all day was tough and wore me out. I enjoyed a week spent with 2 of my favorite people.

It's always great to see to [[Kolt Kane|Kolt]]. We're not the same friends we once were, but it's always easy to connect right back to where we were the last time. Our long history and many shared goals for the future are fuel to a fire of good conversation and a camaraderie.

I learned a few things about myself this week that I had somewhat learned on our Florida [[Family]] Trip last may.

1. I'm such an Introvert. I need my alone time in a major way. That alone time needs to consist of some Deep Work (see Deep Work Book)
2. I love my Kids & Wife. As much as I know I'm such an important part of my kid's life. Ellie and I have a such a special and close relationship. She's learning and growing at a such a quick pace right now. Her need for encouragement and support of that learning is paramount. Although, her needs are not to be above [[Mac]]'s at the moment either. I noticed a definite increase in his speech upon return which is awesome! Also, I'm his only guy in our [[family]] of a female majority. He needs male guidance to grow well. This morning I had tender moment with only [[Poppy|Poppy]] on the bed that was so precious to me. I tickled her, she taunted me, I tickled her, she taunted me, etc. [[Poppy|Poppy]] has much joy to share with the whole [[family]]. [[Andrea]] is such a wonderful woman and I was so glad to be home with her. Also, a week without sex is tough and I was very anxious for it. It started off a bit rocky because I was grumpy about $200 in babysitting but her sanity is important as well. I'll have to continue working on finding that balance. I am looking forward to some face to face time this weekend playing games and enjoying each other.
3. I love my routine. I miss it when I don't have it. I may want it to be in the Pacific Northwest/West Coast/Colorado areas. My daily routine which includes the food the following:
	- The Food I eat
	- Morning Carl & Ellie time with [[Andrea]]
	- Seeing the Kids Grow
	- Walk to Work
	- Learning about Programming
	- Growing the Business
	- Solving problems
	- Yoga
	- Learning about Programming
	- Watching various shows
	- Slowing getting to enjoying Home Remodeling

It's good to reflect on the week and analyze myself a bit. See you tomorrow!
