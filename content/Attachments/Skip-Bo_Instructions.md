# Skip-Bo Instructions

## Objective

Be the first player to play all the cards from your **Stockpile**.

---

## Setup

1. **Players**: 2-6 players.
2. **Cards**: Shuffle the deck thoroughly. Skip-Bo cards are wild and can substitute any number.
3. **Deal the Stockpile**:
	 - Each player is dealt a **Stockpile** of cards:
		 - 30 cards for 2-4 players.
		 - 20 cards for 5-6 players.
	 - Place the Stockpile face down in front of each player. The top card is flipped face-up.
4. **Draw Pile**: Place the remaining cards in the center to form the **Draw Pile**.
5. **Play Piles**:
	 - Four **Building Piles** in the center (shared by all players).
	 - Each player has up to four personal **Discard Piles**.

---

## Gameplay

1. **Starting a Turn**:
	 - Draw **5 cards** from the Draw Pile into your hand.
2. **Playing Cards**:
	 - Use cards from your hand, Stockpile, or Discard Piles to build **Building Piles**.
	 - Building Piles:
		 - Must start with 1 (or a Skip-Bo card) and continue in sequence up to 12.
		 - Completed piles (1-12) are discarded and can start again with 1.
	 - Skip-Bo cards:
		 - Wild and can represent any number.
	 - Play as many cards as possible during your turn.
3. **Ending a Turn**:
	 - If you can’t play anymore, place one card from your hand into one of your **Discard Piles**.
	 - Each Discard Pile is face-up and organized by the player.

---

## Special Rules

- **Skip-Bo Cards**:
	- Wild cards can be used in place of any number.
	- Use them wisely to extend your turn or finish piles.
- **Discard Piles**:
	- You can have up to 4 personal Discard Piles.
	- Cards in these piles can only be used during your turn.
- **Building Piles**:
	- Shared by all players. You can play on any pile.

---

## Winning the Game

- The first player to empty their **Stockpile** wins the game.
- If the Draw Pile runs out, shuffle completed Building Piles to form a new Draw Pile.

---

### Additional Tips

- Use Skip-Bo cards strategically to maintain longer turns.
- Keep an eye on your opponents’ Stockpile cards and try to block their moves.
- Balance playing from your Stockpile and managing your Discard Piles.

---

Enjoy your game! 🃏
