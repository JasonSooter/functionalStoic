## Exercise

### Three Benefits of Working Out

1. **Stress Reduction**: Exercise is linked to better mental health, with people who work out experiencing 43% fewer days with poor mental health, as per Lancet Psychiatry.
2. **Better Sleep**: Regular exercise improves sleep quality, leading to more energy and alertness.
3. **Immune System Boost**: Physical activity helps prevent and manage health issues like stroke, high blood pressure, and arthritis.
